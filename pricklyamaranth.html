Prickly Amaranth

ScientificName:

Amaranthus spinosus

Comman name:

Mullai Keerai

contains:

vitamin A,Iron

Use to cure:

Anemia

Area:

Tamil Nadu

Description

Preparation: Soup: Boil chopped Mullai Keerai with onion and garlic, simmer for 15–20 mins, and drink 1–2 times daily. Stir-fry: Sauté with mustard seeds, cumin, and onion; consume with rice or roti. Juice: Blend fresh leaves with water, add lemon juice, and drink daily. Usage: Consume regularly through soup, stir-fry, or juice to increase iron intake. Advantages: Rich in iron and folate for hemoglobin production. Enhances iron absorption (with vitamin C). Boosts energy and reduces fatigue.
